Company Overview

We are a leading International Freight Forwarders, Customs Clearing Agents, and Freight Consolidators in Saudi Arabia. Could we be privilege to introduce ourselves as "AL JAWHRA TRADING SERVICES EST" having Trade Link round the Globe.

AL JAWHRA TRADING SERVICES EST, having its head office at Saudi/ Dammam, Established in the year 2000, to render ideal Services to Clients. Our network of Chapters and Associates are at all major gateways which help us to reach our Patrons instantly. We are one of the leading International Freight Forwarders, Consolidators and Customs Clearing Agents in Saudi.

Our Agents / Associates spread over in USA, UK, Germany, Singapore, Hong Kong, Australia & Canada, Including several European & Far Eastern Countries. We are tied-up with very strong Freight Forwarding Companies, hence, are capable to offer the optimum services at most competitive rates with the help of our global network of Agents & Associates. For your Information we always use "A" Class Carriers / Liners in keeping with the desire and dictum of our Client.

Our Company is managed by a Board of Directors and has experienced Personnel including Executives for Customs, Docks & Airports operations, Marketing Assistants, Office Assistants, Technical Executives, Accountants, Computer Operators etc.

To be an alert partner of the dynamic Trade, our personnel attend courses with Airlines, Customs, Seaport & Airports Authorities and Computer Software to be updated with rules, regulation & procedure.

Our specialties include handling of Break-Bulk cargo, large Warehouse, infrastructure for handling of Chemicals, Hazardous substance . We have complete setup to handle Ocean, Air and Inland Freights all over Saudi and can render assistance toward Import and Export to and from round the World. Transportation and "Destination - Delivery" are arranged after completion of relevant Customs & Port formalities.
